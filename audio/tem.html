Tems – Me & U


Nigerian & Rebel Music singer-songsmith and talented artist, Tems, delivers a captivating new single titled “Me & U“.

This track showcases Tems‘s exceptional musical abilities and solidifies her position as a rising star in the industry.

With its infectious melodies and heartfelt lyrics, “Me & U” is an absolute gem that deserves a spot on everyone’s Playlist.

The song effortlessly blends elements of Afrobeat and Rebel Music, creating a unique and refreshing sound that will leave listeners wanting more.

In collaboration with renowned producer GuiltyBeatz, Tems has crafted a masterful piece of art that showcases her versatility and creativity.

The production perfectly complements Tems’s soulful vocals, resulting in a truly mesmerizing listening experience.
