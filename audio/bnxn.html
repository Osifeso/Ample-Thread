BNXN Ft. 2Baba – Regret


Nigerian singer-songsmith, BNXN, returns with a brand new track which is titled “Regret” featuring lengendary 2Baba.

This latest offering from BNXN is an amazing record that should definitely find its way onto your Playlist.

The song “Regret” is taken off BNXN‘s highly newly released body of work, “Sincerely, Benson“.

This project is set to be a game-changer in the music industry, and “Regret” is just a taste of what’s to come. With its captivating lyrics and infectious melodies, this track is sure to leave a lasting impression on listeners.

To make this song even more special, BNXN enlisted the help of award-winning music star, 2Baba. 2Baba delivers an amazing verse that perfectly complements BNXN‘s vocals, creating a harmonious blend of talent and creativity.

As for the production, BNXN teamed up with the talented Guilty Beats.
