Bella Shmurda – My Brother (Tribute To Mohbad)


Nigerian singer-songwriter, Bella Shmurda has returned with a highly anticipated Tribute To Mohbad titled “My Brother“.

With its infectious melody and heartfelt lyrics, “My Brother” is an absolute gem that deserves a spot on everyone’s Playlist. The song effortlessly blends elements of Afrobeat and contemporary R&B, creating a sound that is both refreshing and addictive. Bella Shmurda‘s soulful voice shines through, delivering a powerful performance that resonates with listeners on a deep level.

The lyrics of “My Brother” are a heartfelt tribute to the bond between siblings, emphasizing the importance of love, support, and unity between him and Mohbad.

Bella Shmurda‘s poignant storytelling ability is on full display, painting a vivid picture of the unbreakable connection shared between brothers. The song’s relatable message is sure to strike a chord with audiences of all ages and backgrounds.

You guys will surely love this!!
